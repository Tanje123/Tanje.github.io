Important

When making use of this platform make sure to stay on the same internet connection on your mobile device, if you visit the website using a WiFi connection and switch from internet connection while completing an offer a payout will not be given out.

What is the difference between Easy and Normal offers?

Easy offers only require that the application needs to be downloaded and opened for a period of one minute, these offers are very easy to complete.

Normal offers require more than just downloading and opening the mobile application, an example of this would be downloading and achieving a certain level in the application to receive the payout. Normal offers are harder to complete that is why the payout is usually higher.

Can I uninstall an application after I have completed an offer?

Yes it is up to you to keep or uninstall the application after that the offer has been completed.

How can I request a payout?

From the profile page a payout can be requested, all of the available funds on day of the payment will be paid out to the user.

No (new) offers are showing?

We are constantly working on getting offers on the platform, however we cannot always guarantee that this will be the case. If it is possible try using a different mobile device.

Can I complete the same offer multiple times?

No, a payout will only given out for each unique install. When the same offer is done twice only the payout for the first installation will be given out.
